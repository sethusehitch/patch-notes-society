- Separate emissions goals from energy-system implementation.
- Identify the sector and constraint for every claim.
- Use EIA/EPA/DOE/FERC/NERC for U.S. baseline data where possible.
- Treat affordability and reliability as core design constraints, not objections.
- Track built infrastructure and emissions reductions, not announcements alone.
