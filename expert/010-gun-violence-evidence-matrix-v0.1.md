# Patch Notes #010: Gun Violence / Public Safety Evidence Matrix

Version: v0.1 public pre-memo matrix
Date: 2026-07-03
Status: Source-anchor matrix for reviewer critique. This is not a final evidence review.

## Working Standard

Every claim should specify which outcome it addresses: suicide, community homicide, intimate-partner violence, accidental injury, police shooting, mass shooting, school threat, trafficking, illegal possession, or lawful ownership. "Gun violence" is too broad to be a usable evidence category by itself.

## Research Gaps Before Memo v0.2

1. Current firearm mortality by category: suicide, homicide, unintentional, legal intervention, undetermined.
2. Nonfatal firearm injury data quality and limitations.
3. Community violence intervention evidence by model: street outreach, focused deterrence, hospital-based violence intervention, group violence intervention.
4. Domestic violence firearm restriction evidence and implementation gaps.
5. ERPO evidence by suicide, threats, and mass-violence prevention.
6. Safe-storage and lethal-means counseling evidence, especially for youth and suicide prevention.
7. Gun trafficking, illegal possession, and theft data at a non-operational policy level.
8. Constitutional constraints after Bruen and Rahimi, especially for permitting, prohibited persons, domestic-violence restrictions, and sensitive places.
