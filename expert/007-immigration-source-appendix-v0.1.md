## Source Rules For This Issue

- Use official data for counts, categories, and administrative flow where possible.
- Use secondary trackers only when official data is unavailable, delayed, or hard to interpret.
- Separate encounters, admissions, grants, pending cases, removals, overstays, and population estimates.
- Avoid using a single number to imply immigration as a whole.
- Avoid individual legal, benefits, travel, enforcement, or evasion guidance.
