## Source Rules For This Issue

- Separate contributions, expenditures, independent expenditures, lobbying, procurement, revolving doors, gifts, stock trading, and enforcement.
- Use official data for counts/filings where possible, and secondary sources for synthesis and usability.
- Do not call something bribery unless it fits the legal/factual claim.
- Treat constitutional constraints as design constraints.
- Track whether a reform reduces influence, improves disclosure, improves enforcement, or merely moves influence elsewhere.
