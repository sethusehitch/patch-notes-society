## Source Rules For This Issue

- Separate firearm suicide, homicide, nonfatal injury, accidental injury, police shootings, and mass shootings.
- Use official mortality/crime data for baseline counts where possible.
- Use RAND/NIJ/CDC and academic reviews to grade policy evidence.
- Define "mass shooting" explicitly before using counts.
- Treat constitutional constraints as design constraints, not footnotes.
- Avoid tactical weapon detail, attack detail, self-harm detail, crisis advice, legal advice, emergency advice, private facts, or individualized safety guidance.
