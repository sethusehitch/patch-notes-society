## Source Rules For This Issue

- Separate learning outcomes, credentials, attendance, postsecondary outcomes, career outcomes, and civic outcomes.
- Treat NAEP as a core trend measure, not as the entire definition of education.
- Do not use graduation rates without checking readiness, completion, or labor-market outcomes.
- Separate K-12, higher education, career pathways, and adult learning.
- Treat local governance and finance as implementation constraints, not background details.
- Treat AI and education technology as developing evidence areas requiring guardrails.
